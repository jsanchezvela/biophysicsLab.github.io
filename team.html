Dr. Elizabeth Hinde

Principal Investigator

Dr. Elizabeth Hinde is the Principal Investigator of the Laboratory for Cellular Biophysics at the University of Melbourne. Her research focuses on developing and applying advanced fluorescence spectroscopy and live-cell imaging techniques to uncover how nuclear architecture regulates genome function in real time.
